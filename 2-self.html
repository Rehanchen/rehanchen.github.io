Two roads diverged in a wood, and I—
I took the one less traveled by,
And that has made all the difference.

Hi, I am Weijie Chen. I have near 4 years working experience as a research analyst. I have been involved in various programs like user research, social research (such as third-party evaluations), and business analysis. These programs require to adopt qualitative and quantitative methods to collect data, analyze them, and output reports to achieve analytical goals.

Being a memeber of digital products' design has also become an important part of my work experience, including B2B, B2C, and ToG software development; each with different research directions and focuses. These projects have not only given me diverse design experiences and thus enriching a deeper understanding of design, but also often made me wonder: do I doing this right?

It is precisely this conflict that has led me to re-examine my experiences and try to to explore how to make better designs. Perhaps I would find the answer finally.
